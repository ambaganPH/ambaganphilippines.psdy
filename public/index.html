Walang Maliit o Malaking Ambag, Ang Lahat ng AMBAG ay DAKILA


Crisis Risk Management, Relief Operations, & Rehabilitation

Ambagan Philippines is dedicated in helping the Filipinos during the times of crisis, calamity, and/or pandemic. Also, the organization aims to equip the communities the knowledge and skills needed to survive these events.


Pakikipag-ambagan

Ambagan is a popular tagalog word that means contribution. Also at this time, people always ask "Ano ba ang ambag mo?" (What is your contribution?). Ambagan PH answers this question with actions. Relief operations, community pantries, community rehabilitation after a calamity. This is the principle that drives the organization to help because we believe that it doesn't matter if your contribution is small or big, all contributions do matter as long as it is for the betterment of the communities


Turning Beneficiaries into Volunteers

Here in Ambagan Philippines, we turn our beneficiaries into volunteers. You can be a volunteer and help other people thru efforts, time, and love.
